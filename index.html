Why GMNExchange?

With GMN Exchange, you can start selling your products / services to hundreds of millions of customers worldwide. GMNE provides a market floor for you so you can focus on making great products.

What's Included In The Program?

GMN Exchange offers the following:  Easy to join the program  A direct Selling option so that you can ship directly to GMN customers with prepaid shipping  Integration into the GMN Exchange catalog and search  24-hour customer service  24x7 mobile access to your products

Is GMN Exchange Right For My Business?

If your answer is “Yes” to these statements, we believe that GMN Exchange is a good fit for you.  I want GMN to sell my products so I can fulfill orders from my warehouse.  I want GMN to be the seller of my products directly to the customer.  I want GMN to manage customer service and returns for my products.  My product is sold in one of the product categories in Eligible products. If you answered “No” to any of the above, you might want to consider one of our other programs:  Advantage is a self-service consignment program that enables you to promote and sell media products directly on gmnexchange.com.  Selling on GMN Exchange is a program that enables individuals and businesses to sell their products and inventory on gmnxchange.com. You own the inventory and can take advantage of additional GMN Exchange services.

What Is Direct Selling?

Direct Selling is another way of distributing your products to GMN customers. Instead of sending products to GMN Exchange Floor, you send them directly to customers, right from your warehouse. You still sell your products through the GMN EXCHANGE Floor {website,} but when customers order your products, we notify you and provide a prepaid shipping label. You then pack your products and ship them to the customer from your location. GMN still provides 24-hour customer service. Note: In case of a return, your product goes back to GMN Exchange Floor.

How Much Does It Cost?

To join GMN Exchange or continue in the program, we do ask you to send some free units of items that you want to sell on GMN Exchange. You decide which products you want to sell and, based upon the selling price to GMN Exchange of the item, we will request a number of samples. You’ll provide GMN Exchange with both your suggested retail price to customers and the selling price of the product to GMN Exchange. If the selling price of your item to GMN Exchange meets our expected price, then we will accept your price. If it is higher than our expected price, we will recommend a price that we can accept for the item.

What Are The Free Units Used For?

We request a limited number of free units as an investment in the program. We understand that your products cost money, so we want you to know we are using them to build your GMNEXCHANGE.COM product detail page and evaluate customer demand.
